# JobsAI Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2026

JobsAI helps users track job applications, compare compensation, and practice interview Q&A. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we share it, and the choices you have.

## Important Notes About User-Entered Information

Most information in JobsAI is entered voluntarily by you. Job titles, company names, locations, compensation amounts, contacts, notes, job URLs, pasted job descriptions, and interview answers may be incomplete, inaccurate, outdated, fictional, or otherwise not verified by JobsAI.

JobsAI uses user-entered information to operate the app and generate job-search-related features, including application records, compensation estimates, interview Q&A, key points, transcripts, feedback, and funnel views. JobsAI does not verify user-entered information, does not make hiring decisions, and does not provide background checks, employment screening, credit decisions, insurance decisions, or other eligibility determinations.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you create or use an account, we may collect your name, email address, user ID, sign-in provider, authentication status, and basic profile preferences such as desired role, target location, years of experience, and target compensation.

### Optional Profile and Job Search Content

You may choose to enter job application details such as company name, job title, job location, application status, compensation details, job URLs, hiring manager or recruiter names, notes, pasted job descriptions, and other job-search information. These fields are optional except where the app identifies a field as required to create or use a specific feature.

### Location Information

JobsAI is designed to use city/state-level, city/region-level, or remote-work information for compensation, tax-estimation, and job-search context. We do not require street addresses and do not intend to collect precise GPS location, home address, or continuous location tracking. If you type a location manually, the information may be inaccurate or fictional. If you choose a location suggestion, Google Places may process the query according to Google's terms and privacy policy.

### Interview Practice and Voice Content

If you use Q&A practice or voice feedback, we may process interview questions, key points, recorded answers, transcripts, scores, and feedback. Voice recordings are used to generate transcript-based feedback. Unless the app clearly states otherwise, JobsAI does not intend to store raw voice recordings as permanent account content after processing.

### Purchase Information

We use RevenueCat and Apple App Store in-app purchases to manage subscription status, purchase history, restore purchases, and entitlement access. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

### Analytics and Diagnostics

We collect limited usage analytics and diagnostic data to understand product performance, improve features, and investigate crashes. This may include app platform, app version, subscription access tier, feature usage events, device context, error reports, and similar technical information. We do not use this information to make employment, eligibility, credit, insurance, or hiring decisions.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide account access and sync saved data
- Save, display, and update job applications
- Generate job-search-related outputs from information you provide
- Estimate compensation, taxes, deductions, and take-home pay
- Generate and display interview Q&A practice, key points, transcripts, scoring, and feedback
- Provide application funnel and progress views
- Manage subscriptions, paywall access, and restore purchases
- Improve app reliability, safety, performance, and product quality
- Respond to support, privacy, or account requests
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

## How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers that help us operate JobsAI. These providers are authorized to process information only as needed to provide services to us, subject to their own terms and privacy obligations.

JobsAI may use the following service providers:

- Supabase for authentication, account data, and database services
- RevenueCat for subscription entitlement management
- Apple App Store for in-app purchases and subscriptions
- Firebase AI Logic / Gemini for AI-assisted processing
- Google Places for city/state or city/region location suggestions
- PostHog for product analytics
- Sentry for crash and error diagnostics

We do not sell your personal information. We do not sell user-entered job search content. We do not share user-entered job search content for cross-context behavioral advertising.

## AI Processing

Job descriptions, job URLs, application context, interview questions, answer recordings, and transcripts may be sent to AI services to generate job-search-related outputs, including Q&A practice, key points, transcripts, scoring, and feedback.

AI outputs may be incomplete, inaccurate, outdated, or unsuitable for your situation. Do not enter sensitive personal information, confidential employer information, trade secrets, government identifiers, health information, financial account information, or other information that is not needed for job search or interview practice.

## Data Retention

We retain account and app data for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce our terms, and improve reliability. Some information may remain in backups, logs, analytics, or diagnostic systems for a limited period after deletion from the main app experience.

You may request deletion of your account or app data by contacting support.

## Your Choices

You may:

- Edit or remove job applications and notes in the app
- Choose whether to enter optional job, compensation, contact, location, and voice-practice information
- Manage or cancel subscriptions through Apple
- Restore purchases through the app
- Avoid optional voice practice
- Contact us to request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy assistance

## State Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive information about personal information we process. You may also have the right to opt out of certain sharing or targeted advertising. JobsAI does not sell personal information or share user-entered job search content for cross-context behavioral advertising.

To exercise privacy rights, contact us using the email below.

## Children

JobsAI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

## Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## International Use

JobsAI is operated from the United States. If you use JobsAI outside the United States, you understand that information may be processed in the United States or other locations where our service providers operate.

## Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are material, we will update the effective date and may provide additional notice in the app or through other reasonable means.
